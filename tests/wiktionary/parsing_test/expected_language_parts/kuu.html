Finnish[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈkuː/
  • Hyphenation: kuu
  • Rhymes: -uː

Etymology 1[edit]

From Proto-Finnic *kuu, From Proto-Uralic *kuŋe. Cognates include Estonian kuu and Hungarian hold.

Noun[edit]

kuu

  1. moon, satellite (of a planet)
  2. month

Etymology 2[edit]

From Proto-Finno-Ugric *kuje.

Noun[edit]

kuu

  1. (dialectal) animal fat, fat, tallow, especially in reindeer
