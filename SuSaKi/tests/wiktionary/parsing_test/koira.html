Finnish[edit]

Finnish Wikipedia has an article on:
koira

Wikipedia fi

Etymology[edit]

From Proto-Finnic *koira, from Proto-Uralic *kojra, *kojera, from Proto-Uralic base *koj(e)- ‎(“male, human”). Compare koiras.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈkoi̯rɑ/
  • Rhymes: -oirɑ
  • Hyphenation: koi‧ra

Noun[edit]

koira

  1. dog
  2. dog paddle (swimming stroke)
  3. (military slang) military police

Synonyms[edit]

  • (dog):
    • (archaic) peni
    • (baby talk) hauva (doggy)
    • (old, poetic) hurtta (hound dog)
    • (derogatory) rakki, piski, murre, tiisti (mongrel)
    • (dialectal, humorous) hunttu
  • (swimming stroke):
    • koirauinti
  • (military police):
    • sotilaspoliisi
