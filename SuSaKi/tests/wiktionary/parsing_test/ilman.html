Finnish[edit]

Preposition[edit]

ilman (+ partitive)

  1. without:
    • ilman rahaa = without money

Synonyms[edit]

  • -tta (the abessive case suffix, gives the same meaning, "without")

Noun[edit]

ilman

  1. Genitive singular form of ilma.

Anagrams[edit]

  • liman
