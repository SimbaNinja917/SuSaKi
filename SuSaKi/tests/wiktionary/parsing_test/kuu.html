Estonian[edit]

Etymology 1[edit]

From Proto-Finnic *kuu, from Proto-Uralic *kuŋe. Cognates include Finnish kuu and Hungarian hold.

Noun[edit]

kuu ‎(genitive kuu, partitive kuud)

  1. moon
  2. month
